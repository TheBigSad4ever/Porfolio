Helldivers 2

Helldivers 2 takes place in a science fiction universe,
in which humanity is now united under the "Managed democracy" of Super Earth.
The Helldivers are elite soldiers sent by Super Earth to fight the Galactic War and protect the planet and humanity from various alien and foreign threats.
